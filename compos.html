General rules

  • open web technologies only (webGL, canvas, ...). No plug-ins, no extras.
  • the production should not have been released previously
  • the production have to work on at least one major webbrowser (IE, Firefox, Chrome). You are allowed to experiment on the nightly build of Firefox or Chrome, but please keep us informed.
  • the organizing team reserve the right to disqualify entries that are inappropriate (pornography, illegal content, ...).
