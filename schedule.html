How I learned to shrink my Javascript demos

Technical description of JS compression tricks, based on PNG-embedded source file (french speaking / english slides).
by Charles Boccato, computer engineer at Qobuz.com and developer since his early childhood. In the demoscene, he is known as cb from the Adinpsz demogroup and is the coder of the DemoJS 2012 invitro and the recent 'JsExe' compression tool.
» http://adinpsz.org

JSSpeccy: The toy project that wasn't

Matt will explain the challenges of writing a ZX Spectrum emulator in Javascript, and how recent developments in the language and browser technologies have turned it from a toy project into a serious rival to conventional desktop-based emulators - and how Javascript might even turn out to be the best tool for building the next generation of emulators.
by Matt Wescott. Matt "Gasman" Westcott has been active in the demoscene for many years, starting out on the Spectrum, and released the first ever full-scale Javascript demo at Assembly 2004. In his day job, he's a front- and back-end developer for UK web agency Torchbox.

Demoscene and kids

What demoscene productions you should show to your kids (french speaking).
by Jean-François Richard, aka JeFfR3y, old-school demoscener, co-founder of the AmigaVibes portal and member of the Vitalmotion demogroup.
» http://amigavibes.org
