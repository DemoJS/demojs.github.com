Demoscene? Demoparty?

The demoscene is a computer art subculture that specializes in producing demos, which are audio-visual presentations that run in real-time on a computer. Demosceners are coders, graphic artists, computer musicians... (more info on wikipedia)

A demoparty is an event that gathers demosceners and other computer enthusiasts to compete (more info).

DemoJS demoparty is a demoparty specialized in web technologies. We welcome both experienced demosceners and people who have never been to a demoparty.

If you are a beginner in web technologies, here are some links to help you. Also, you will find help at the party place, don't hesitate to ask people around you!

Demo JS

Started as one of the Mozilla worldwide demoparties in 2011, DemoJS is the parisian web only demoparty. It has been initiated by @gatuingt, with the help of core Paris JS, Popsy Team members and other demosceners and Mozilla folks. It now takes place every year at the end of june.
