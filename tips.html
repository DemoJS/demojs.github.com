How to meet musicians and graphic artists?

If you are a lonely coder looking for a soundtrack or some graphics for a demo/intro, you can probably find a demoscener willing to collaborate with you.

Try:

  • the "Petites annonces" section of Demoscene.fr (in french)
  • the pouet.net BBS: create a new topic with an explicit title (eg: "Looking for a musician for DemoJS", "Looking for a demo soundtrack"), and ignore the trolls
  • #revision on IRCnet

Need more help?

  • Demoscene.fr BBS (in french)
  • pouet.net BBS
  • Feel free to drop us an email: contact@demojs.org.
  • Also, you can probably find help at the party place, don't hesitate to ask people around you!.
